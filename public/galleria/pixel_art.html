My process: I use aseprite and start with a 32x32 or 64x64 canvas and place the pixels (1px) one by one. I then use this pixel art scaler to make the images bigger.

Annotations:I still have to learn about anti-aliasing and a lot of other stuff. :O
Pixel art is incredibly fascinating and surprisingly useful to learn how to simplify images and values, a skill that can be easily transfered to other art practises.
It's also incredibly relaxing.
