„ÄåADD-ON„Äç		 The only add-on everyone needs to install.

„ÄåADD-ON„Äç		 You can filter and block unwanted channels and keywords from YouTube.

„ÄåADD-ON„Äç		 Productivity assistant. Blocks any site you want for any time you specify. A little bit difficult to set up in the beginning, but when you get how it works, it's an essential tool to have.
