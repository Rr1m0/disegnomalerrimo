Dicembre 2023

Nuove abitudini a fine anno

Hello, everyone! It's December!!!
Do you like December and all the Christmas music and decorations?!
I'm mostly indifferent to all of it. Christmas itself doesn't excite me that much. It's like... a day.
I like the red, though. Red is a good colour!

One thing I have to say is: The weather is terrible here where I live and it's freaking cold in my house. I have like 15° celsius in my studio if I'm lucky.
My hand are always freezing and drawing is difficult. I have to spend at least 10 minutes trying to warm my hands... sigh.
On the good side: I've been sleeping a lot. I love it. I love sleeping. Sleeping is the best.

Anyways! It's almost the end of the year! And what do you do at the end of the year, usually? Start a new habit, of course!
One thing that I've been doing lately is drawing just before going to sleep.
I usually read a book or a fanfiction on my ereader while I'm laying on my bed, but I had the idea of putting a sketchbook I was not using and some brush pens (Kuretake ZIG Clean Color Real Brush) that I was not using on my bedside.

I suffer from chronic neck pain so I can't draw a lot while I'm sitting on my bed because of the awkward position, but I've been drawing for a little bit every night for a couple of weeks now and so far I'm loving it!
It's usually mindless stuff like drawing my cat who's sleeping beside me or drawing about movies or books I'm currently enjoying.

I've never been much of a writer, so even if I try journaling on a daily basis I usually end up writing a few sentences and then forgetting to update it for a week. (writing also strains my neck and right shoulder more than drawing)
But with this new drawing journal I can just mess around on the pages, think about my day and relax. Just like journaling, but without words.
It's nothing novel and I bet a lot of people already do that, but I'm surprised of how much it benefits me.

So yep, drawing silly doodles before sleeping? I totally recommend it.

All is well all is good, but let me share my last random stuff with you! (yeah, I attempted a rhyme because I'm awesome like that)
